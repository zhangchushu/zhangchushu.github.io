Biography

I received my Master’s degree from the National University of Defense Technology (NUDT) in 2024, advised by Professor Wei An. My research interests lie in Computer Vision, especially Event-based Vision, and large language model training/inference tasks — including inference acceleration and adaptive corpus construction.
