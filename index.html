Biography

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Xueyang Fu, Zheng-Jun Zha, and Qibin Sun. I received my Master’s degree from the National University of Defense Technology in 2024, where I was advised by Professor Wei An.
My research interests include Computer Vision (low-level vision, event-based vision) and Large Language Models (training/inference acceleration, adaptive corpus construction).
Previously, I have been a research intern at Shanghai AI Laboratory with Xiaowei Hu and at vivo with Bo Li.
